Khwopa Secondary School

KSS was founded by Bhakapur Municipality on 16th Shrawan, 2056 (1st August, 1999). Buoyed up with the idea of enlightening the whole society and the nation by means of providing quality education, the municipality made an aggressive initiation realizing their long term vision of the over all development of Bhaktapur.

Compulsary Subjects:

  1. Business Studies
  2. English
  3. Account
  4. Nepali

Optional Subjects:

  1. Mathematics
  2. Computer Science
